About the firm


Shaleen Consultants was founded in 2004 by Ar. Pawan Mishra. We are a multidisciplinary architectural, planning and interior design firm with extensive experience in both new construction and renovation. Most of our work tends to be for Residential and Commercial (Group Housings, Mixed used Towers and commercial Complex/Malls) . We understand these types of clients and their needs, and have a proven ability to develop consensus and empower leaders to make good decisions. Doing so has enabled us to serve these markets very effectively and has brought us much success.

Founder and Principal Architect - Pawan Mishra (B.Arch, IIA)

Architect Pawan Mishra is known as the Liasoning expert of Uttar Pradesh.After starting his work in the field of architecture in 1994 he worked at various firms in Lucknow before setting up his firm Shaleen Consultants in 2004.His main goal is to keep all his clients satisfied with his work and that is why many notable architects and real estate developers are his regular clients.

Ar Pawan Mishra gives equal importance to both small and big projects and takes equal interest in designing them.With an experience of more than 25 years in this field of architecture he is easily able to address all types of clients from private builders to government authorities.

Principal Architect - Yati Kumar Mishra
(B.Arch, Pune)

Gold medalist in architecture.Yati Kumar Mishra is a young architect who is keen in improving the society through design by always thinking of apt solutions required for the betterment of people. He does not believe in following a single philosophy of design whereas he tries to experiments with different materials and forms as per required context.A firm believer of sustainable and green architecture he always tries to minimise the carbon footprint of the projects.

Yati has worked under many reputed architects in Lucknow and Pune. Before joining Shaleen Consultants he interned at Arch-en-design under Ar Ashok Kumar where he gained experience in institutional and cultural projects.He has also worked under Ar Rajat Kant and Ar Ashwini and Sandeep Pethe where he learned more about place making in public spaces.
